The pace of it

    By species

      These are land animals killed for food, counted as individuals. They do not include fish and other aquatic animals — by number the largest group of all, killed in the hundreds of billions to trillions — nor animals who die before slaughter, the day-old male chicks culled at hatcheries, or animals killed outside recorded food production. The true toll is higher than what is shown here.

      The pace assumes the year's killing is spread evenly across it; the live figures are a steady rate, not a live feed. The FAO "China" aggregate is excluded so its regions are not counted twice.
